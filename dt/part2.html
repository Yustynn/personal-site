Welcome to Griddy World!

Move around the grid to collect as many points as you can. You have a limited number of moves.

There are 3 kinds of cells you can encounter: Nothing, Monsters, or Treasure.

fiber_manual_record

Nothing will give you 0 points.

mood_bad

Monsters give you -10 points.

monetization_on

Treasure gives you 10 points.

Each cell has a different probability of encountering each of the 3.
Each cell also displays the occurrences of each encounter you have faced in that cell.

Hitting the reset button will reset your score and position, but the probabilities stay the same.
